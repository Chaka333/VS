Recipe for Stir fry

Ill be showing you a simple stir fry that'll take minutes to make

Ingridients
  • day old rice

    • i know but it makes sure the rice doessn't stick together

  • 3 eggs whisked
  • vegetable blend(your choice)
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1t minced ginger
  • 1/2c light soy
  • 1/4c oyster sauce
  • 3T butter
  • 2# protein of your choice

Procedure

The trick here is to layer the flavors

  1. in a wok or Sautee pan heat up 1/4c oil to med heat
  2. add your garlic and ginger and cook until fragrant
  3. add in your protein and cook half way
  4. add in your eggs, toss
  5. add in your vegetables and heat through
  6. add in rice and cook through
  7. add in liquids
  8. add in butter to finish

And *chefs kiss Done!
